Blood Clots

In the United States 900,000 people are affected by blood clots (VTE) a year.3

Embedded content: https://youtu.be/7lJfh00F7Zg

A blood clot forms when blood cells clump together and change the blood flow in a vein.456

Symptoms:

  • Pain
  • Swelling
  • Discoloration (blue or red)
  • Warmth 456

Pulmonary Embolism (PE)

A pulmonary embolism (lung clot) is when a blood clot (venous thromboembolism) moves to the lungs.456

Symptoms:

  • Shortness of breath
  • Chest pain
  • Unexplained cough
  • Unexplained rapid heart rate. 4568

Blood Clot Prevention:

Embedded content: https://youtu.be/vNArtKdLPC8

1. What will be done to prevent blood clots?4568

2. Will you or the patient be taking a blood thinning medication?4568

3. Make sure the patient's compression stockings and boots (sequential compression devices) are plugged in and working while the patient is in bed or sitting in a chair.12

4. Only walking is not enough to prevent blood clots.45

5. Aspirin alone will not prevent blood clots.7
